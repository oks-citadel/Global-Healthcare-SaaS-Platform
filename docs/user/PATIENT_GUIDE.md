# Patient User Guide

Welcome to Unified Health! This guide will help you get started with the platform and make the most of your healthcare experience.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Visit** https://app.unifiedhealthcare.com
2. **Click** "Sign Up" or "Get Started"
3. **Enter your information:**
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - First and last name
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
4. **Verify your email** by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. **Complete your profile** with additional health information

### Logging In

1. Go to https://app.unifiedhealthcare.com
2. Enter your email and password
3. If enabled, complete two-factor authentication
4. You're in! Welcome to your dashboard

## Your Dashboard

Your dashboard provides a quick overview of your health:

- **Upcoming Appointments** - See your scheduled visits
- **Recent Activity** - View your health records and test results
- **Messages** - Communicate with your healthcare providers
- **Health Insights** - AI-powered health recommendations
- **Quick Actions** - Book appointments, refill prescriptions, upload documents

## Booking an Appointment

### Step 1: Choose Appointment Type

1. Click **"Book Appointment"** from your dashboard
2. Select the type of care you need:
   - **Virtual Visit** - Video consultation with a provider
   - **Phone Consultation** - Audio-only consultation
   - **In-Person Visit** - Visit a clinic or hospital
   - **Specialist Consultation** - See a specialist (cardiology, dermatology, etc.)
   - **Mental Health Therapy** - Therapy or counseling session
   - **Health Checkup** - Comprehensive health screening

### Step 2: Select Provider and Time

1. **Choose a provider** or let the system recommend one
2. **Select a date and time** that works for you
3. **Review available slots** (green = available, red = booked)

### Step 3: Provide Information

1. **Reason for visit** - Briefly describe your symptoms or concern
2. **Upload documents** - Include any relevant medical records or images
3. **Medical history** - Update your health information if needed

### Step 4: Confirm and Pay

1. **Review appointment details**
2. **Select payment method:**
   - Credit/debit card
   - Insurance (if applicable)
   - Subscription plan
3. **Confirm booking**
4. **Receive confirmation** via email and SMS

## Virtual Consultations

### Before Your Appointment

**15 Minutes Before:**
- You'll receive a reminder notification
- Test your camera and microphone
- Find a quiet, well-lit space
- Have your ID and insurance card ready

**Joining the Call:**
1. Click **"Join Video Call"** from your appointments page
2. Allow camera and microphone access
3. Wait in the virtual waiting room
4. Your provider will join when ready

### During Your Consultation

- **Be clear and specific** about your symptoms
- **Ask questions** - don't hesitate to clarify
- **Take notes** if needed
- **Request a summary** of the visit

### After Your Appointment

- **Review visit summary** - Check notes and recommendations
- **View prescriptions** - Download or send to pharmacy
- **Schedule follow-up** if recommended
- **Rate your experience** - Help us improve

## Managing Your Health Records

### Viewing Your Records

1. Go to **"Health Records"** in the main menu
2. Browse by category:
   - **Visits** - Past consultations and encounters
   - **Lab Results** - Blood tests, imaging, diagnostics
   - **Prescriptions** - Current and past medications
   - **Immunizations** - Vaccination history
   - **Allergies** - Known allergies and reactions
   - **Conditions** - Diagnosed conditions and chronic diseases

### Uploading Documents

1. Click **"Upload Document"**
2. Select document type:
   - Lab results
   - Imaging reports
   - Vaccination records
   - Medical history
   - Insurance cards
3. Choose file (PDF, JPG, PNG)
4. Add description and date
5. Upload

### Sharing Records

**With Healthcare Providers:**
1. Go to **"Health Records"**
2. Click **"Share"** on any record
3. Select the provider or facility
4. Choose what to share
5. Set expiration date (optional)
6. Send

**Downloading Records:**
- Click **"Download"** on any record
- Choose format (PDF or FHIR)
- Save to your device

## Prescriptions

### Viewing Prescriptions

1. Go to **"Prescriptions"** in the menu
2. See all current and past prescriptions
3. Check status:
   - **Active** - Currently taking
   - **Pending** - Waiting for pharmacy
   - **Completed** - Finished course
   - **Cancelled** - No longer needed

### Refilling Prescriptions

1. Find the prescription
2. Click **"Request Refill"**
3. Confirm pharmacy
4. Submit request
5. Get notification when ready

### Sending to Pharmacy

1. Select prescription
2. Choose **"Send to Pharmacy"**
3. Search for pharmacy or select from favorites
4. Confirm and send
5. Pharmacy will notify you when ready

## Health Checkup Programs

### Booking a Health Checkup

1. Click **"Health Checkup"** from dashboard
2. Choose your package:
   - **Basic Health Screening** - Essential tests
   - **Comprehensive Checkup** - Full body assessment
   - **Executive Health Package** - Premium screening
   - **Cardiac Package** - Heart health focus
   - **Women's Health** - Gender-specific screening
   - **Men's Health** - Gender-specific screening

3. Select date and location
4. Review included tests
5. Confirm and pay

### During Your Checkup

You'll go through various stations:
- **Registration** - Check-in and paperwork
- **Physical Exam** - Vitals, BMI, examination
- **Laboratory** - Blood and urine samples
- **Imaging** - X-rays, ultrasound, mammogram, etc.
- **Specialist Consultations** - Based on findings
- **Summary** - Review with physician

### Getting Your Results

- Results available within 3-7 business days
- Notification when ready
- View in **"Health Records"** section
- AI-powered health insights included
- Schedule follow-up if needed

## Payments and Billing

### Payment Methods

Add and manage payment methods:
1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Payment Methods"**
2. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
3. Enter card details or select alternative:
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Bank Transfer
   - Mobile Money (in supported regions)
   - Insurance

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **"Billing"** > **"Invoices"**
2. See all past charges
3. Download or print invoices
4. Track payment status

### Insurance

**Adding Insurance:**
1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Insurance"**
2. Click **"Add Insurance"**
3. Enter insurance details:
   - Provider name
   - Policy number
   - Group number
   - Upload insurance card
4. Submit for verification

**Using Insurance:**
- System automatically checks coverage
- Shows your estimated out-of-pocket cost
- Submits claims automatically
- Track claim status in **"Billing"** > **"Claims"**

## Subscription Plans

### Viewing Your Plan

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Subscription"**
2. See current plan details:
   - Plan name
   - Features included
   - Renewal date
   - Cost

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
2. Compare available plans
3. Select new plan
4. Review changes
5. Confirm upgrade
6. Changes take effect immediately

### Adding Family Members

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Family"**
2. Click **"Add Family Member"**
3. Enter their information
4. Choose their access level:
   - **Full Access** - They can manage their own account
   - **Dependent** - You manage for them
5. Confirm

## Privacy and Security

### Your Privacy Rights

- **Access** - View all your data
- **Export** - Download your complete health record
- **Delete** - Request account deletion
- **Restrict** - Limit who can see your information
- **Object** - Opt out of certain uses

### Data Sharing Consent

1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Privacy"**
2. Review and manage who can access your data:
   - Healthcare providers
   - Family members
   - Research (opt-in)
   - Marketing (opt-out)

### Two-Factor Authentication

**Enable 2FA for Extra Security:**
1. Go to **"Settings"** > **"Security"**
2. Click **"Enable Two-Factor Authentication"**
3. Choose method:
   - Authenticator app (recommended)
   - SMS code
4. Scan QR code or enter code
5. Verify and save backup codes
