# Unified Health - App Store Listings

## App Information

**App Name:** Unified Health

**Short Description (80 chars):**
Secure healthcare management - appointments, records, telehealth & prescriptions

**Full Description:**

Unified Health is your comprehensive healthcare companion, providing secure access to all your health services in one place.

**Key Features:**

• **Appointments** - Schedule, manage, and receive reminders for all your healthcare appointments
• **Medical Records** - Securely access your health records, lab results, and imaging reports
• **Telehealth** - Connect with healthcare providers through secure video consultations
• **Prescriptions** - View and manage your medications with refill reminders
• **Secure Messaging** - Communicate directly with your care team
• **Health Dashboard** - Track your health metrics and wellness goals
• **Family Access** - Manage healthcare for family members (with consent)
• **Billing** - View and pay bills, manage insurance information

**Security & Privacy:**

Your health information is protected with bank-grade security:
• End-to-end encryption for all data
• Biometric authentication (Face ID / Fingerprint)
• HIPAA-compliant infrastructure
• No selling or sharing of personal health information

**Languages Supported:**
English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Chinese (Simplified), Arabic
