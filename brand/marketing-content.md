## 10. Compliance Notes

### FDA Guidelines

- No claims of treating, curing, or diagnosing conditions
- Platform does not provide medical advice
- Users directed to healthcare providers for medical decisions

### FTC Guidelines

- All claims substantiated
- Clear distinction between platform capabilities and medical services
- Transparent about data practices

### HIPAA Marketing

- No use of PHI in marketing without authorization
- Clear consent language for communications
- Opt-out mechanisms in all marketing emails
